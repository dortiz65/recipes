Ingredients:
 - 1 cup sugar
 - 4 eggs
 - 4 egg yolks
 - 1 quart half-and-half
 - 2 teaspoon vanilla

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. 

 2. Heat half-and-half to 200 deg F. 

 3. In a large bowl, combine the sugar and eggs.
    Slowly add half-and-half. 

 4. Pour mixture into 12 6oz ramakins. 
 
 5. Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until a center custard set. 
 
 5. Cool in refrigerator overnight.
